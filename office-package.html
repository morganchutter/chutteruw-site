What’s covered

Property and liability, bundled.

One package covering the office’s property and commercial liability risks — with broad-form property protection and the extensions a professional office actually needs.

Commercial General Liability

  • Medical fees
  • Legal expenses
  • Other related costs, such as replacement of damaged property
  • Personal injury (libel, slander, false arrest, malicious prosecution)
  • Medical payments
  • Tenant’s legal liability
  • Non-owned automobile coverage

Commercial Property

  • Broad-form coverage for flood, sewer backup, and earthquake on office contents
  • Option to add Extra Expense

Property extensions of coverage

Theft damage to buildings$2,500
Currency, money, cheques & stamps$500
Personal effects$500
Property away from premises$2,500
Valuable papers & records$2,500

Good to know

Program exclusions.

A few exposures this package doesn’t cover — place these elsewhere or speak to us.

Cyber & Data BreachCyber and data-breach exposures are not covered under this package.
USA Sales & OperationsUS sales and operations exposures are excluded.
Professional LiabilityThis is a property & CGL package — professional liability (E&O) is placed separately.
