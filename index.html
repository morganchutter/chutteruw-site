About

Built by underwriters,
for brokers.

For 30 years, Chutter has supported Canadian brokers with practical, broker-first underwriting. We’re a boutique MGA built for speed, service, and flexibility — broad coverage, fast turnaround, and A-rated Canadian paper.

From high-hazard contractors to accounts that just don’t fit a rigid box, we’re known for taking a thoughtful look — and finding answers where others won’t. Independent, responsive, and relationship-driven.

We write Commercial General Liability, Excess & Umbrella (including commercial auto fleet excess), Professional Indemnity, and Commercial Property — with broad in-house authority.

No Contracts. No Minimums.

Start placing business with us today. No appointments. No paperwork. No volume thresholds.

Full Commission. Yours to Keep.

Competitive commissions on every line. We respect the broker relationship — your client stays your client.

Decisions, Same Day.

Broad in-house authority means the desk handling your account can quote it. No head-office lag.
