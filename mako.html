How it works

From submission to bound — in five steps.

The whole flow runs at quotemako.com. Brokers sign up free — no contract, no minimums.

1

Create your account

Sign up at quotemako.com. Licensed Canadian retail brokers only — verify your licence and you’re in.

2

Drop the broker package

Upload whatever you already have — a completed application PDF, the broker’s email (with attachments), an ACORD form, or a mixed bundle. Mako pulls every field that matters.

3

Review the AI risk summary

Mako returns a structured risk summary in seconds — operations, revenue, exposure, location, loss history. Correct anything that needs correcting; nothing is bound until you confirm.

4

Pick a limit & options

Choose the CGL limit and any optional coverages (tools floater, etc.). Mako prices it instantly and shows conditions and extensions attached to the quote.

5

Bind & issue

One click to bind. The invoice and policy documents generate immediately — download them or email them to your insured. The whole quote history lives in your dashboard.

FAQ

Common questions.

Which lines does Mako quote today?

Mako quotes Commercial General Liability (CGL). More lines are on the roadmap — if you have a specific line you’d like to see, let us know.

Do I need a contract with Chutter to use Mako?

No. Any licensed Canadian retail broker can sign up at quotemako.com. No contract, no minimums, no volume commitments.

What happens if a risk is outside Mako’s appetite?

Mako routes the submission to a Chutter underwriter for a manual look. You get a real underwriter’s response — not a dead-end error message. The same team you’d have emailed anyway.

What if Mako gets a field wrong?

Nothing is bound until you confirm. Every field Mako extracts is editable in the risk summary before you request pricing. Correct anything that’s off, and Mako re-rates immediately.

Where does bound Mako business live?

Every bound policy sits on Chutter’s book, on A-rated Canadian paper. Same insurer relationship, same claims team, same everything you already know from placing with Chutter.

Is my broker and client data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to your broker workspace — other brokers can’t see your quotes or bound business.
